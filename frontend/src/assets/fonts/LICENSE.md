These fonts are bundled locally (rather than loaded from Google Fonts CDN) to avoid any
external network requests at runtime. The privacy guarantee of this application —
that no data is transmitted to any third party during use — requires eliminating
external font CDNs that would otherwise log your IP address and usage times.
